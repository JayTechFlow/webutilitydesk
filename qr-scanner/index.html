QR Scanner

Scan a QR code from your camera or an uploaded image when the browser supports image barcode detection.

Ready
This tool needs a browser with `BarcodeDetector` support for QR scanning.

Use cases

Quickly decode QR codes from phones, flyers, screenshots, and test images.

Camera scans

Point the camera at a QR code and read the decoded value without a separate app.

Image scans

Upload a screenshot or saved image to decode the QR content from your desktop.

Browser support

Native scanning works best in modern browsers that expose the `BarcodeDetector` API.
