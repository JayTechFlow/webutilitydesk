Word Counter questions.

What does a word counter count?

It counts words and usually shows related text stats like characters, lines, sentences, and paragraphs.

Does this tool count browser data?

No. It only counts the text you paste into the page.

Why use a word counter?

It helps with essays, blog posts, summaries, social copy, and content limits.
