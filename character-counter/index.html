FAQ

Character Counter questions.

What does a character counter count?

It counts characters and often includes related stats like words, bytes, and lines.

What is the difference between characters and bytes?

Characters count visible text units, while bytes depend on the text encoding and can be larger than the character count.

Does this tool count spaces?

Yes, the character total includes spaces unless you use the no-spaces count.
