FAQ

MD5 Generator questions.

What is MD5 used for?

MD5 is commonly used for checksums, quick integrity checks, and legacy systems that expect an MD5 hash.

Does this tool upload my text?

No. The hash is generated locally in your browser.

Can MD5 be reversed?

No. MD5 is a one-way hash and should not be treated as encryption.
