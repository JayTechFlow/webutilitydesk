FAQ

Password generator questions.

What length should I use?

Use at least 16 characters for normal accounts and longer passwords for highly sensitive systems.

Is the password saved?

No. The page does not provide accounts or storage, and the generated value stays in the browser page.

Can I generate multiple passwords?

Yes. Use the secondary action to create five passwords with the selected length.
